Beautiful Film Output
In 1986, while Omnibus owned Digital Productions, we used the Digital Film Printer to print this image from a Samsung commercial Digital Productions had done. I still have a 4x5" color transparency that is gorgeous! This image was scanned from a color lithograph printed in the Omnibus Annual Report of 1986.

If anyone can provide me with credits from the DP crew, I will gladly list their names here. Who knows who actually owns the copyright to it.
